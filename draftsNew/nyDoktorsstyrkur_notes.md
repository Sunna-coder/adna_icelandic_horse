# Nýdoktorsstyrkur — University of Iceland Postdoctoral Grant
## Application Notes

**Funder**: University of Iceland (Háskóli Íslands), Division of Science and Innovation
**Contact**: rannsoknasjodir@hi.is
**Grant page**: https://english.hi.is/research/funds-and-grants/internal-grants/post-doc-grants

---

## Key facts

- Up to **15 grants** awarded per round; at least 2 per School
- Sunna's School: **School of Social and Human Sciences** (Faculty of Social and Human Sciences)
- Duration: up to **3 years**
- **2025 deadline**: May 8, 2025 (round closed)
- **2026 deadline**: Not yet announced — expected around April–May 2026. Check the grant page from March 2026 onwards.

---

## How to apply

Unlike Rannís, this is **not a Word template** — the application is submitted through an **online form**:
- Portal (2025): soloforms.lausn.is/input?fid=809&lang=is
- The 2026 form will be linked from the grant page when the round opens

---

## What the application requires

1. **CV and cover letter**
2. **Research proposal** — must include: innovation, value, objectives, and a public description
3. **Publication list**
4. **PhD diploma** (copy), or a letter from supervisor confirming the defence date (if not yet graduated)
5. **Statement of support** from the relevant University of Iceland school/faculty/institute — a `.docx` template is available from the grant page; request it from rannsoknasjodir@hi.is
6. **Two expert reference letters** — sent as PDFs directly to rannsoknasjodir@hi.is by the referees (not included in the application form)
